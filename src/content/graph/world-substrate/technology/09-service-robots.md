# Service Robots

Many services are not fundamentally digital. They are physical acts coordinated by intelligence.

Cleaning, moving, repairing, cooking, delivering, inspecting, assembling, and caring all require bodies. AI can plan these services before it can perform them. Robots complete the circuit: the agent leaves the screen and acts in matter.

The service robot economy is therefore not separate from AI. It is AI with limbs, sensors, tools, and liability. The product is no longer a dashboard that tells a worker what to do. The product is a system that does the work.

This changes the meaning of labor. Human work stops being the default way services enter the world.
