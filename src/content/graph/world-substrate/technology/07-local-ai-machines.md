# Local AI Machines

The future home may contain not only appliances, but a private computational center.

This machine hosts models, stores memory, coordinates robots, manages permissions, and controls productive devices. It is not only a computer for screens. It is a domestic command layer for autonomy.

Cloud models will remain powerful, but total dependence on remote intelligence makes the individual fragile. A local AI machine lets a person keep memory, preferences, and everyday control close to the body and home. It becomes the household's nervous system.

The question is whether such machines become common property, luxury equipment, or infrastructure that every person is expected to possess.
