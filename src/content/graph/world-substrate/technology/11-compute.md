# Compute

Compute becomes one of the basic materials of Neo-Koinonia.

It is not merely server capacity. It is the substrate through which agents reason, generate media, run simulations, coordinate robots, search designs, and operate production systems.

When software is cheap, compute remains scarce. The valuable question is no longer only what can be coded, but what can be run, at what cost, with what latency, under whose control, and with what guarantees.

Compute is the heat of intelligence made economic.
