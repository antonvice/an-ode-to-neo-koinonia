# Extended Mind

The extended mind is moving from notes and memory into interface.

Voice dictation already shows the beginning of this shift: the human does less manual manipulation and more intention transmission. The computer becomes a surface that listens before it becomes a surface that is clicked.

BCI would deepen the pattern by moving from spoken language toward latent intention. It would make the boundary between thought and command thinner, though also more dangerous.

A2UI completes the loop by returning not only words, but an interactive surface the mind can keep shaping. The mind extends outward, the agent acts, and the world returns as editable interface.
